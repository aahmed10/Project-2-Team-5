ABOUT PROJECT NAME

Do you have stacks of printed recipes sitting on your counter? Are you overwhelmed with the number of recipes that you have bookmarked (and might have forg0tten)? That’s where we come in! Project Name is an app where you can store all the recipes you come across while browsing the internet. The app allows you to search through your recipes, organize ones that you’ve cooked or ones that needs to be cooked, edit recipes to your liking, and mark ingredients that you have or still need to get.
